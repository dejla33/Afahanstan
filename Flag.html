History of Afghanistan's Flag
1. Early Flags (1800–1901)
Flags in this era were mostly black and simple, symbolizing power. There were no complex emblems used.
2. Amanullah Khan Era (1919–1929)
After independence, a tricolor flag (black, red, green) was introduced, with mosque symbols in the center.
3. Republics & Political Change (1929–1992)
Various regimes changed the flag, introducing socialist symbols like stars, wheat, and gear wheels.
4. Mujahideen & Islamic Identity (1992–2001)
The tricolor flag was brought back with more religious symbols including the Shahada and Islamic architecture.
